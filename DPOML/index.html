Our History, Our Heritage

Dantelle Palm Oil Mills (Nig) Ltd was commisioned 1952 and established under the umbrella of New Nigeria Devolpment Company (NNDC). In Patnership with Easton and Johnson England Industries, DPOML was established to serve the colonial economy - the oil palm produced was exported. It continued until 1076, when the management fell under the late Muhammed Dantelle under the administration of muritalla/Obasanjo regime ( 1976-77)

Dantelle Palm Oil Mills (Nig) Ltd, early beginnings, had no plantation at that moment, so DPOML sourced for raw materials from Local producers. This trend continued even as Late Muhammed Dantelle as owner and founder who also relied relied on raw materials from Local producers and government agencies such as Benue State Government, Aloma Plantation, Asharo Plantation, until his demise 1987, the management fell - the company became mouribourne until 2007, when Yusuf Dantelle started the company as Dantelle Palm Oil Mills (Nig) Ltd Inc [2007]

        In a bid to continue with the earlier trend, the management seemed it not feasible because of the increased population in the country and the awareness of palm oil production, and it's derivatives.

          So, We (DPOML) had to improvise into oil palm plantation and development 2009/2010. The first plantation started 17th July, 2011 @ Okabo, Oganaji and Ogbadu all in Dekina Local government of Kogi State Nigeria.  Cummutatively between 2011-2016, we've established over 100 hectares of plantation; while in the plantation development, materials were sourced from local producers with the aim of bridging the gap of the gestation period and not to solely depend on them (local producers) as a source of raw materis. 

July 2018, An MOU was undertaken with Kogi State University to oversee and manage her palm oil plantation for the period of 5years.

Currently, we're developing a virgin land of 520 - 1000 hectares of land for oil palm plantation. Our mill is fed by raw materials from our own farm plantation.

Our mill machine is basically driven by a steam turbine - we do not rely on electricity/petroleum machine. Hence, we recycle our waste products - palm kernel shells and fibres to fire the boiler that generates the steam for the entire plant

Our Vision 2020 is to be fully independent of raw materials, Thereby expanding into palm oil crushing, palm oil cakes, sales of  nursery bed oil pam trees, palm oil fruits, palm oil, palm kernels, fibres, palm kernel shells, consulatncy. Our vision also to provide employment opportunities for 2000 citizens and also provide scholarship to school children of Dantelle Palm Oil Mills (Nig) Ltd. environs.

It's our pleasure to serve you better!

Management Profile

Leveraging on the training/exercise from his palm trade background, Alhaji Yusuf M. Dantelle incorporated Dantelle Palm Oil Mills (Nig) ltd. with the family plant which has been in existence since 1952 and embarked on the establishment and development of oil palm plantation to palm plantation to provide sufficient raw materials for the plant. The goal is to establish and sustain an agro-allied palm processing business across the value chain in Anyigba/Kogi and Nigeria at Large. This will contribute in bridging the gap in the short fall in the demand for oil palm produce in both local and external market - export markets, stimulate the generation of employment (skilled & unskilled, direct & indirect). The goal is in tandem with the federal government goal of reclaiming our lost fame in the oil palm industry and sustainable agricultural development as a key driver of the Nigerian economy.

Alhaji Yusuf M. Dantalle

CEO/MD [ Dantelle Palm Oil Mills Ltd.]
